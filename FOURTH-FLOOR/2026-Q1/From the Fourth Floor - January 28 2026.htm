Hello Deese College Community, 

Like the beloved holiday classic, we have truly been walking in a "Winter Wonderland." I hope that you and your family have stayed safe and warm during the recent inclement weather. The campus is getting back to normal.  Please exercise caution in the parking lots and sidewalks as icy patches still remain in shaded places.  Additionally, students may experience difficulty in arriving to campus due to icy conditions and may need additional consideration with attendance.  I appreciate your flexibility and understanding during the academic and operational changes this week.

The weather forecast predicts additional snow and precipitation this weekend.  Stay tuned to University updates as conditions change quickly.  Feel free to contact me or your Department Chair with any logistical questions over the coming days. 

Enjoy the winter scenery and I look forward to seeing you on the yard soon. 

Have a great day!

Regards, 

Dean Smith
