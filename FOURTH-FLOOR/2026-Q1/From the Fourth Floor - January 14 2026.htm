Simple Syllabus Mandatory Training Sessions are in full swing to prepare for compliance with the new UNC System Office regulation.  See the message from the Office of the Provost below.  The deadline for Spring 2026 submission is January 23, 2026.  The University expectation is to have 100% compliance in this effort.  Please feel free to contact me or your Department Chair with any questions.  Your prompt attention to this task will be greatly appreciated.  If you have any implementation questions or concerns, please share them using the comment button below.   Your feedback will be helpful with future implementation guidance from the Provost.  

 
Simple Syllabus: Mandatory Training Sessions
The UNC System Office has implemented a new regulation—Regulation on Publication of Academic Calendars, Grading Policies, and Related Materials—designating syllabi as public records. Beginning with the 2026–27 academic year, all syllabi must be publicly accessible online. To prepare for this transition and ensure compliance, faculty are required to use Simple Syllabus during the current semester (Spring 2026); however, syllabi will not be published publicly at this time.  The attached instructions will assist you in preparing your course syllabi using the new platform. For further support in this process, please plan to attend one of the mandatory training sessions beginning next week. Sessions will be recorded.

Craig Hall and Merrick Hall Painting and Installation
 

University Facilities and Identity Group staff will be on-site this semester to install the Willie A. Deese and Dean Emeritus Quiester Craig recognition walls on the 1st floors of Craig and Merrick Hall.  This installation is a follow up to the college naming and is a partnership between the Deese College and University Development.  The installation is expected to be complete by March 2026.  I think you will be pleased with the outcome when finished. 

During the installation process, please exercise caution while navigating the 1st floor hallways.  If you have any questions, please contact Tyeisha Bell-Bullard, College Building Manager.
