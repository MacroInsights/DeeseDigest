Hello Deese College Community, 

Welcome to February!  This month is filled with opportunities to celebrate historical achievements and to celebrate the spirit of love.  In the Deese College, we remain committed to advancing excellence and opportunity, and we celebrate the role that our community plays in carrying this legacy forward.  During the month, I will feature prominent individuals from our Deese College history that have contributed to the legacy that we stand upon.
