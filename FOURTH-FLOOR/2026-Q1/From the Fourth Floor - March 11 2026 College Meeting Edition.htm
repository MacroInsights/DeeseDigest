CANVAS FALL 2026

 

North Carolina A&T State University will transition from Blackboard to Canvas in Fall 2026. ITTD has a wealth of resources to assist in the transition to the new LMS platform. 

  • The Canvas LMS Implementation website is located here: 
    https://hub.ncat.edu/administration/its/ittd/canvas-implementation.php
  • Our own Dr. Megan Weber and Xamora Reed are serving as Faculty and Student Canvas Champions. 
  • The Deese College has an assigned instructional designer, David Whitehead (dfwhitehead@ncat.edu or 285-4488), to assist with implementation questions.  
  • ITTD is hosting a number of workshops to prepare for the Fall 2026 launch.  Refer to the March ITTD schedule for more information. 

Dr. Tracie Lewis from ITTD will be our featured guest speaker during the March College Meeting and will discuss the new LMS rollout in further detail.

WCAG 2.1 Level AA Accessibility Standards Compliance

April 24, 2026


By April 24, 2026, all government websites, third-party services, and digital course content must meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA accessibility standards. This requirement ensures equitable access for all users, including those with disabilities. - NC A&T Digital Accessibility Resource Hub 

Digital Accessibility Resource Hub - https://www.ncat.edu/provost/academic-affairs/aggiesteach/accessibility1.php 

Blackboard Accessibility Report LTI 1.3 - Click the link in the Blackboard Course Tools to get a quick snapshot of your course content accessibility score.
