# DeeseDigest — Project Instructions

## What This Is

The **Deese Digest** is the external-facing quarterly newsletter of the **Deese College of Business and Economics at NC A&T State University**. It is the public communications product of the college, distinct from the internal weekly newsletter "From the Fourth Floor." Its purpose is to increase the visibility of the college and leverage the brand recognition of NC A&T with an audience of alumni, Triad business community members, and university stakeholders.

## Source Material

- **Content source:** `FOURTH-FLOOR/<year>-Q<N>/` — weekly internal newsletters, organized by quarter. These are the raw material for each Digest issue. Filter out all internal action items (deadlines, meeting logistics, IT notices, compliance reminders) before use.
- **AEI data source:** `https://macroinsights.github.io/piedmont-triad-aei/aei-current.json` — generated by the AEI pipeline each month and pushed to GitHub Pages. Contains all banner fields pre-computed: `index_value`, `momentum`, `labor_utilization`, `regime`, `avg_growth_3m_pct`, `blurb`, and `sparkline` (last 8 end-of-quarter values). Use this as the single source of truth — no manual number lookup needed.
- **AEI blurb source:** Use the `blurb` field from `aei-current.json` directly. Adapt tense or phrasing for the quarter if needed but do not rewrite the structure.
- **Brand assets:** `IMAGES/Deese-Logo.png` — used in header and footer of every issue.

## AEI Banner — Option C (Locked)

The AEI is the crowning jewel of the Digest. It appears in a full-width gold banner immediately below the hero image. It does not compete with other content — it is a fixed anchor feature every quarter.

**Layout:** Index value + supporting pills + blurb text on the left; 8-quarter sparkline on the right.

**Data to populate each quarter:**

| Element | Source |
|---|---|
| Index value | Last month of quarter, `index_2019` column, latest nowcast CSV |
| Momentum pill | Last month of quarter, `growth_momentum` column, rounded to 1 decimal |
| Regime + avg growth pill | Derived from 3-month average of `index_growth`; regime thresholds: Expansion > +0.2%, Contraction < −0.2%, Flat otherwise |
| Sparkline | 8 end-of-quarter `index_2019` values (last 8 quarters) |
| Narrative blurb | Lifted from page 1 of the latest AEI monthly PDF (`../AEI/REPORT/`) and adapted to the quarter's end month |

**Sparkline construction:** Use end-of-quarter months (March, June, September, December). Map values to SVG y-coordinates: `y = 65 - ((value - min) / range) * 60`. Last point is highlighted with a larger filled circle. Past points are smaller with reduced opacity.

**No nowcast disclosure needed.** The AEI pipeline no longer produces forecasts — all values in `aei-current.json` are based on complete BLS observations only.

## Image Workflow

Photos are not embedded in the repo. The process for each quarterly issue:

1. When pulling content from the Fourth Floor, send one request to the college communications office for: one event photo per major story, headshots for any faculty/student spotlighted, and one strong hero image for the top.
2. Drop received photos into `FOURTH-FLOOR/<year>-Q<N>/IMAGES/`.
3. If a photo does not arrive, the template uses a styled placeholder: blue circle with white initials for headshots; blue gradient box with gold text for image slots. Do not leave empty boxes.
4. Hero image suggested source: the most visually compelling event of the quarter (e.g., Student Recognition Gala, speaker series event, commencement).

## Delivery

- **Format:** HTML email via Mailchimp (free account to start).
- **Cadence:** Quarterly.
- **Audience:** Alumni (primary), Triad business community, university stakeholders.
- **Tone:** Professional and celebratory — closer to USF MUMA than Rice Wisdom. Short blurbs, not long-form articles. Accessible to non-economists.

## Rules

- **Always ask before making changes to more than one file at a time.**
- The canonical template lives at `MOCKUPS/deese-digest-q1-2026.html`. Each new issue gets its own file: `MOCKUPS/deese-digest-q<N>-<YYYY>.html`.
- Never merge Research Highlights and People into a single section — they serve different audiences and should remain separate.
- The AEI blurb is always sourced from the official AEI monthly PDF, not written from scratch. Adapt the numbers; do not rewrite the structure.
- The Dean is always **Dr. Kecia Williams Smith** unless explicitly updated.
