From the Dean

As we close out the first quarter of 2026, I am proud to share the momentum building across every corner of the Deese College. Our faculty continue to publish groundbreaking research, our students are earning national recognition, and our partnerships with Triad industry leaders are opening doors that were unimaginable just a few years ago.

This issue marks the inaugural edition of the Deese Digest — our quarterly window into the life of the college and the health of the regional economy we study, serve, and help shape. I hope you find it informative, and I invite you to stay connected.

Dr. Kecia Williams Smith
Dean, Deese College of Business & Economics · NC A&T State University

Faculty in the National Spotlight
Deese Economist Takes AI Conversation to National Radio

Dr. Scott Simkins, professor of economics at the Deese College, was featured on Marketplace — the nationally syndicated public radio program hosted by Kai Ryssdal and heard by millions of listeners daily. Dr. Simkins spoke to how artificial intelligence is reshaping the way macroeconomics is taught at the undergraduate level, and what that means for the next generation of business professionals entering an AI-driven economy.

His appearance signals the growing national profile of Deese College faculty and the college's commitment to being part of the most important conversations in business education today.
